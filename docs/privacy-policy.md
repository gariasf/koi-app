# Koi — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 16 June 2026**

Koi is a car-companion app for iPhone. It is built to keep everything on your
device. There is no account, no sign-in, and no Koi server, so there is nothing
for us to collect, see, or sell.

This is the whole policy. It is short because Koi does so little with your data.

## What stays on your device

Your cars, plans, costs, fuel logs, photos, reminders, and any insurance details
you enter are stored only on your iPhone, and in your own iCloud device backup if
you have iCloud Backup turned on. That backup is managed and encrypted by Apple and
is accessible only to you. Koi's developer cannot read it.

Photos you add are downscaled and stripped of their location/EXIF metadata before
they are saved.

## What leaves your device

Only two things ever leave your device, and both happen **directly from your phone
to a third party, never through a Koi server**:

1. **Fuel prices.** Your chosen region (a Spanish province code) is sent to the
   Spanish government's public fuel-price service (the *minetur* open-data feed) to
   fetch local prices. The request contains no information that identifies you.
2. **Directions.** If you tap to get directions to a fuel station, the station's
   address is handed to your maps app (Apple Maps or, if installed, Google Maps),
   which then operates under its own privacy policy.

We do not run a server in between, and we do not log these requests.

## Location

If you grant permission, your location is used **only while the app is open** and
**only** to show the cheapest fuel near you. Your location is never stored and is
never sent to the developer.

## No tracking, no ads, no analytics

Koi contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking SDKs. It does
not track you across other apps or websites, and it requests no advertising
identifier.

## Data we collect

None. The developer of Koi does not collect, receive, store, or process any personal
data about you. (On the App Store this is declared as **"Data Not Collected."**)

## Your control and your rights

Because your data lives on your device and we hold nothing, you are always in direct
control of it:

- **Export** everything as a file: *Settings → Export my data*.
- **Erase** everything from the device: *Settings → Delete all data*.

If you are in the EU/EEA or the UK, data-protection law gives you rights to access,
correct, and delete your personal data. Since the developer holds none of your data,
there is nothing for us to retrieve or erase on your behalf. You exercise these
rights directly in the app, using the controls above. Removing the app also removes
its data from your device.

## Children

Koi is a general-audience app and is not directed at children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published here and shown inside
the app under *Settings → Privacy*.
